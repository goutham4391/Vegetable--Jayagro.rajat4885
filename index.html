Welcome to Jay Agro-Export

Jay Agro Export is one of the leading fresh fruit, vegetable export company from India.

Our main activity is export of Table grape, Pomegranate, Onion, Mango & Fresh Vegetables.  For quality production Bayer Crop science and jay agro export have entered in to partnership to produce highest quality table grape and vegetables according to global gap standard and good agricultural practice.

Geographical location: We are Located at Nashik (India) which is the only place in India where 80% of grapes, onion & vegetables are exported. Nashik has highest cultivation and production of grape, pomegranate, onion, and vegetables. Nearest sea and air port is MUMBAI located only 4.00 hour journey from Nashik. This is the major benefits of our geographical location.

Infrastructure

We operate through our modern pack house / cold storage facility for grapes, onion grading center & modern packing facility for Vegetable in Nashik (India) with Best Hygienic conditions.

Quality Assurance

Being quality conscious organization ,all our produce from associated growers are closely monitored in various growth stages till packaging, the produce can be tested in APEDA recognized laboratories as per customer’s need.

Packing

Jay Agro Export Packing is carried out in APEDA Approved pack house in hygienic packaging material to suit end users requirements for export as well as domestic market.
